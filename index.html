About Us

Welcome to The Flower Queen Workshop

We have been spreading the joy of flowers for over 7 years. Our dedicated team of floral designer have a combined experience of over 36 years and love creating beautiful, stunning arrangements for delivery all over Israel.

We are an online florist and gift business and proudly 100% Israeli owned and operated. We pride ourselves with supporting the local community and Israel economy by keeping our product as locally sourced as possible. This ensures you receive the freshest quality bouquets and arrangements, backed up by our 7 Day Freshness Guarantee.

We also send a picture of the bouquet we have made on your behalf as your personal reassurance of quality. This way, you can see exactly what we have designed for your special gift, and be assured that what you have ordered online is what was sent. You can trust The Flower Queen to bring your feelings to life through the flowers you send.
